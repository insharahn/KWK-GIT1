My Coding Journey

My coding journey actually began right here at Kode With Klossy two years ago during the summer of 2023 when I joined the Data Science Summer Camp as a Scholar. It was during a time that I was unsure of what degree to pursue for my undergraduate degree; I was torn between Electrical Engineering and Computer Science.

However, the time I spent as a Scholar helped me a lot as it offered me a glimpse into the expansive world of programming. That month I spent playing around with Python, SQL, and Looker Studio eventually led to me deciding to pursue Computer Science, and the rest, as they say, is history!

As of today, I am fluent in C++, C, SQL, Assembly, HTML, CSS, and have dabbled in Python, C#, Javascript, Shell script, and more. I've built projects ranging from video game recreations to comprehensive databases with multiple interfaces. It's been a tough journey so far, but a rewarding one!
